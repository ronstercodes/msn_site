WHAT IS MATH AND SCIENCE NIGHT?

Created by three high school clubs joined together, Math and Science Night is a fun-filled evening where younger students can explore various math and science topics. This event features 200+ interactive booths led by both students and external organizations, offering hands-on activities and engaging demonstrations.
